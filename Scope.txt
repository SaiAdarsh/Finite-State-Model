1. Our model used as traffic controller to manage intersections of car-car and
car-pedestrian, and car-pedestrian-emergency in two way directions.
2. The signal in NS/EW directions can be one of the three colors {RED,
YELLOW, GREEN} at anytime.
3. The pedestrians can request to walk to other side of the road using the button.
